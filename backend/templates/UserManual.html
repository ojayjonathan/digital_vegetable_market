User Manual

1. How to Register

  1. Open the app and click on the "Sign Up" button.
  2. Enter your phone number in the provided field.
  3. Enter your email address in the provided field.
  4. Enter your first name in the provided field.
  5. Enter your last name in the provided field.
  6. Create a password for your account and confirm it in the provided fields.
  7. Click on the "Register" button to complete the registration process.
  8. You will be logged into the app and can access its features.

2. How to Login

  1. Open the app and click on the "Login" button.
  2. Enter your phone number in the provided field.
  3. Enter your email in the provided field.
  4. Click on the "Login" button to complete the registration process.
  5. You will be logged into the app and can access its features.

3. How to Reset the Password

  1. Open the app and click on the "Login" button.
  2. On the login page, tap on the "Forgot Password" link.
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account and tap on the "Submit" button.
  4. Check your email for a message from the vegetable market app with a password reset link.
  5. Click on the password reset link in the email to open the password reset page on the app.
  6. Enter your new password in the designated field and confirm it by entering it again.
  7. Tap on the "Reset Password" button to save your new password.
  8. You can now use your new password to sign in to the vegetable market app.

Fig 1.2 Login Page Screenshot

It is important to choose a strong, unique password for your account to protect your personal information and prevent unauthorized access to your account. Avoid using easily guessable passwords, such as your name or birthdate, and consider using a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords for all of your online accounts.

3. How to Make a Purchase.

  1. Open the app and create an account by providing your personal information and creating a username and password.
  2. Once your account is created, log in to the app using your username and password.
  3. Browse the app's catalogue to find the goods you want to buy. You can use the app's search function to quickly find specific items, or you can browse by category to see the full range of products available.
  4. When you find an item you want to buy, tap on it to view more details and to see available opxions, such as size, colour, and quantity.
  5. Select the opxions you want and tap on the "Add to Cart" button. You can continue shopping and add more items to your cart, or you can proceed to checkout.
  6. To checkout, tap on the "Cart" icon in the app and review the items in your cart. You can remove items and change quantities.
  7. When you are ready to complete your purchase, tap on the "Proceed to Checkout" button and provide your shipment and payment information.
  8. Review your order details and confirm your payment to complete the purchase. The app will provide a confirmation of your order and an estimated delivery date.
  9. You can track the status of your order in the app and contact the online store if you have any questions or concerns.

4. How to Cancel an Order.

  1. Open the E-market app on your device.
  2. Log in to your account using your phone number and password.
  3. Go to your account's "Orders"  section to view the details of your past orders.
  4. Locate the order that you want to cancel and tap on it to view the order details.
  5. On the order details page, look for a "Cancel" or "Cancel Order" button and tap on it.
  6. A pop-up window may appear asking you to confirm the cancellation. If so, tap on the "Confirm" button to proceed with the cancellation.
  7. The app will confirm that your order has been cancelled and provide any relevant information, such as any refund that may be issued.
  8. You can also contact customer service if you have any questions or issues with cancelling your order.

It is important to note that the availability of the cancel button and the cancellation process may vary depending on the status of your order. Some orders may not be eligible for cancellation or may incur a cancellation fee. It is recommended to check the policies and terms of service before placing an order.

5. How to Create or Update Shipping Adress.

  1. Open the mobile app on your device.
  2. Log in to your account using your phone number and password.
  3. Browse the store's catalogue and add the items you want to purchase to your cart.
  4. When you are ready to checkout, tap on the "Cart" icon in the app and review the items in your cart.
  5. Tap on the "Proceed to Checkout" button to begin the checkout process.
  6. On the checkout page, you will be asked to select a shipping address. If you have multiple shipping addresses saved in your account, you will be able to choose from a list of opxions.
  7. If you want to use a new shipping address that is not saved in your account, tap on the "Add New Address" button and enter the new address information.
  8. Select the shipping address you want to use and tap on the "Continue" button to proceed to the next step in the checkout process.
  9. The app will confirm the selected shipping address and allow you to review and confirm the rest of your order details.

Note: You can select an address by either moving the map location under “Location Marker” or using a search bar on top of the screen.

6. How to Update Profile.

  1. Open the app on your device and log in to your account using your phone number and password.
  2. Go to the app's “Account” section to access your account settings.
  3. Locate the opxion to edit your profile or personal information and tap on it to open the editing page.
  4. Update the fields that you want to change, such as your name, email address, or profile picture.
  5. Review your updates to make sure they are correct and tap on the "Update" button to apply the changes.
  6. The app or website will confirm that your profile has been updated and will show the updated information in your account.

7. How to Add New Product.

  1. Open the app on your device and log in to your account using your phone number and password.
  2. Go to the app's “Account” section to access your account settings.
  3. Tap on the "Add Product" button to open the product creation page.
  4. Enter the product's details, such as its name, category, price, and descripxion. You may also need to upload photos or other media to showcase the product.
  5. Select any additional opxions or settings, such as the product's availability, shipping opxions, or return policy.
  6. Review the product details to make sure they are correct and tap on the "Save" or "Publish" button to add the product to the online store.
  7. The app or website will confirm that the product has been added and will show it in the store's catalogue, where it can be purchased by customers.
